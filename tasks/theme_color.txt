GrowthLab branding and color palette
Primary brand colors
1. GrowthLab Teal (primary)

* HSL: 187 78% 26%
* Usage: Primary brand color, buttons, accents, gradients
* Variations:
* Darker: #0A5A5E / #0d5d61 (hover states)

* Hex: #1E293B
* HSL: 222 47% 11%

* Variations:
* Light: #334155

3. GrowthLab Orange (accent)
* Hex: #F5A623
* HSL: 43 96% 56%
* Usage: Secondary buttons, highlights, featured badges

Brand identity summary
Color psychology
* Teal (#0F7377): Trust, growth, innovation
* Slate (#1E293B): Professional, stability, sophistication
* Orange (#F5A623): Energy, creativity, action
Design principles
* Minimalist and clean
* Professional and modern
* Accessible contrast ratios
* Smooth animations and transitions
* Consistent spacing and typography
Visual style
* Gradient backgrounds (Teal → Slate)
* Glass effects with backdrop blur
* Elegant shadows with teal tint
* Hover effects with scale and color transitions
* Rounded corners (0.5rem to 2rem)
